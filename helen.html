Helen Minerva Lewis

Daughter of Polly Hull Lewis and Daniel Lewis
Granddaughter of Warren and Polly Hull
1828-???

Helen was married twice. She married Leonard S. Hubbard in 1850, but he died in 1855. She married Samuel S. Sumner in 1864. He was a Baptist minister and served as an army chaplain. After the Civil War, Samuel worked for the Internal Revenue Service.
