Aurilla Hull

Daughter of Warren and Polly Hull
1805-1898

Aurilla was the eleventh daughter of Warren and Polly. She was born on October 10, 1805. She was first married to a man named Reid. She later married Robert Upton Wheelock on January 7, 1863, the same day her sister Miranda married William Conley. Robert was a farmer. They had four children. Many of their family members are buried in the Hull Family Cemetery. She died on February 8, 1898.
