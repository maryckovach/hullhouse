Theodocia Hull

Daughter of James Hull
Grandaughter of Warren and Polly Hull
1824 - ???

Theodocia was born on August 5, 1824. She was the third child and only daughter of James and Betsy Hull. She married Henri St. Clair in 1844. Their son, Henry Hull St. Clair, was an important New York City newspaper publisher in the late nineteenth century.
