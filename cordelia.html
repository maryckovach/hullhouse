Cordelia Saphronia Lewis

Daughter of Polly Hull Lewis and Daniel Lewis
Granddaughter of Warren and Polly Hull
1819-1859

After her marriage, Cordelia lived in Ohio. She was an abolitionist, active in the anti-slavery cause. She married Jared Smalley and settled in Ohio. In 1855 she earned her degree in obstetrics. She practiced medicine, delivering babies and treating women's diseases, until she died on August 14, 1859.
