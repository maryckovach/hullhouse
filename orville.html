Orville Duane Howe

Son of Sophia Hull
Grandson of Warren and Polly Hull
1831-1917

Orville was the child of Sophia Hull and Eber Howe. He was born on September 1, 1831. He married Elizabeth Pepoon, who was said to be very fashionable, original, and a great writer of poems and stories. They had two children. Orville was a schoolteacher and superintendent of the Madison, Wisconsin schools. He was also involved with agriculture and politics. He died on February 4, 1917 from grippe, which turned into pneumonia. He died in Nebraska.
