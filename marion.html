Marion Hull

Son of Edmund Hull
Grandson of Warren and Polly Hull
1840-1864

Marion was a soldier in the Freeman's Regiment Missouri Confederate Cavalry, which was attached to the 49th Tennessee Infantry Regiment in 1864. He was wounded in a battle near Fort Stevens in Washington, D.C. and died on August 3, 1864. He was buried in the Tennessee Shiloh Military Park.
